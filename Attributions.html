Jimena Fuentes-Arias

Team leader

Why are you in iGem?

Since I heard about it in 2016 I loved the idea, it was all about changing the world which is all I ever wanted to do. iGEM is an opportunity to explore complex projects which only fit in this competition, it allows to push the boundaries of synthetic biology (my favorite area of biotechnology because of its limitless possibilities) and it involves interdisciplinary work which, for me, its fundamental. Also, getting to know people from all around the world is great.

Something you learned in iGem
